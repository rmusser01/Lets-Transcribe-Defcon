- Introduction: Paul Clip, a consultant at AdStake, presents on hacking using Palm OS-based PDAs, including a demo of a vulnerability scanner called "Austin."
- Past Tools: A review of existing security, communication, and development tools for Palm OS, with a focus on older devices running Palm OS 4 and below.
- Present Tools: An overview of "Austin," a vulnerability scanner designed for Palm OS, including a live demo of its features and functionality.
- Future Tools: A discussion of potential future enhancements to "Austin," including NASL scanning and custom IP packet creation.
- Other Mobile Devices: A brief mention of tools developed by Ollie Whitehouse for the Sony Ericsson P800 mobile phone, including NetScan and PDAZAP.
