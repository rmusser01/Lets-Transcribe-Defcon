In this talk, the speaker, a security expert with experience in testing firewall and IDS products, offers insights into evaluating and improving the effectiveness of these tools. The discussion centers on open-source utilities like Nmap, Whisker, and ISIC, which can be used to test IDSes and firewalls. The speaker emphasizes the importance of understanding how these tools work rather than using them blindly. They also provide suggestions for testing specific aspects of firewalls and IDSes, such as state tables and packet filtering. Additionally, the speaker offers coding tips for designers and developers to avoid common mistakes and improve the security of their products.
