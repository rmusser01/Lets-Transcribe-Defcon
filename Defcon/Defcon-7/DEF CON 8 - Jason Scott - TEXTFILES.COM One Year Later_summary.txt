Jason Scott, the creator of textfiles.com, a website dedicated to archiving bulletin board system (BBS) texts from the 1980s, discusses his experiences running the site. He talks about the various threats and challenges he has faced, including legal threats, copyright issues, and deep linking. Scott also shares his concerns about potential government censorship of online content related to bomb-making and drug use. He emphasizes the importance of preserving historical BBS texts and encourages attendees to create backups of his site to ensure its preservation.
