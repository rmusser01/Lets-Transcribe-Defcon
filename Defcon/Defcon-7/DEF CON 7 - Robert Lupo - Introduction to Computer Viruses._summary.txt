Robert Lupo, also known as "Virus," presents an introduction to computer viruses at DEF CON 7 in Las Vegas, Nevada, in 1999. He covers different types of computer viruses, including boot sector, file infectors, multipartite, and macro viruses. Lupo explains how viruses work, how they spread, and the potential damage they can cause. He emphasizes the importance of backups and safe practices when downloading files. Lupo also discusses false alarms, fakes, and the challenges of virus detection and removal. The talk concludes with a Q&A session, addressing topics such as Unix viruses, email attachments, and virus writers' motivations.
