Gail Thackeray, a Maricopa County Prosecutor from Arizona, and Kevin Higgins, from the Nevada Attorney General's Office, led a Q&A session at DEF CON 7 in 1999. The pair offered legal advice and answered questions on a range of topics, including:
- Nevada laws
- The Electronic Communications Privacy Act
- Hacker Kevin Mitnick's trial
- Employer/employee privacy rights
- Consent and privacy in communications
- Search warrants and arrests
- Forfeiture and equipment confiscation
- Civil liability for system administrators
- Consent and privacy in online conversations
- Anonymous tips and search warrants
- Disclosure of information by websites and ISPs
- Evidence-gathering and computer forensics
