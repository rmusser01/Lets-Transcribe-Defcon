At DEF CON 8, held in Las Vegas in 2000, Space Rogue introduces Freaky, who presents on the security and hacking of macOS and details of OSX. Freaky discusses various software and applications related to Macintosh security, including WacMac Archives, DoorStop firewall software, SoftRouter for internet connection sharing, FreedomSoftware for secure email and encryption, and MacPork for network security auditing. He also mentions Seek and Destroy 4, a war dialer application, and highlights the lack of new releases in Macintosh hacking software. Freaky provides an overview of security and hacking tools, such as password crackers and remote administration software, and offers a CD with Mac files, text documents, and applications. The presentation includes a demonstration of OSX and addresses questions about file sharing, port blocking, and packet sniffing.
