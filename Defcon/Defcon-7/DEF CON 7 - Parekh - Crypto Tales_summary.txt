A speaker, Samir, shares stories from the crypto industry, including his experiences with starting an internet privacy company, C2Net, and the challenges faced by other companies in the industry, such as RSA and Peanut.fi, in navigating relationships with the NSA and dealing with legal issues. Samir also recounts incidents involving the Church of Scientology and the Software Publishers Association (SPA), highlighting the importance of anonymity and security in online activities. The story of a Forbes article about tax avoidance in the crypto community and the speaker's unexpected appearance on the magazine cover concludes the talk.
