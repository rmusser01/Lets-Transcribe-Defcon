Robert Sell, founder of Trace Labs, presents techniques and tools for open-source intelligence (OSINT) gathering, with a focus on locating missing persons. He emphasizes the importance of OPSEC and provides a comprehensive overview of the entire process, from technical setup to target acquisition and various online resources for information gathering. Robert also shares examples of successful Trace Labs operations and discusses target evasion tactics. Finally, he explores the potential future of crowd-sourced OSINT, raising ethical considerations about privacy and convenience.
