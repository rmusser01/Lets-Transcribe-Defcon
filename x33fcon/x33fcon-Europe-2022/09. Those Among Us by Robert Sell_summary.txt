The talk focuses on the growing issue of insider threats, emphasizing the diverse motivations and methods of malicious insiders. Insider threats encompass accidental breaches, intentional data theft, and external manipulation. Robert Sell highlights the need for organizations to implement preventive measures, including training, corporate culture improvements, and technical controls. He suggests strategies such as gamified user awareness training, employee engagement programs, and data loss prevention (DLP) systems. Additionally, he emphasizes the importance of detecting behavioral indicators of compromise and fostering collaboration between security, HR, and legal departments. The presentation concludes with a call to action, urging organizations to develop comprehensive insider threat strategies and take proactive measures to safeguard their valuable information.
