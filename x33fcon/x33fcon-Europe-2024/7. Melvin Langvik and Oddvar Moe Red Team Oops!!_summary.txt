In this talk, "Red Team Oops!!", Melvin Langvik and Oddvar Moe share their experiences and lessons learned from their Red Team operations within Fortune 500 companies. They emphasize the importance of perseverance, creativity, and learning from mistakes. Langvik discusses his challenges with phishing attempts, eventually finding success by targeting pre-created computer accounts with legacy knowledge. Moe shares his initial access struggles, finding a solution through QR code phishing, and introduces his tool, Crucible, for generating QR codes in emails. They encourage a simple approach and highlight how rage and failure can fuel creative research ideas.
