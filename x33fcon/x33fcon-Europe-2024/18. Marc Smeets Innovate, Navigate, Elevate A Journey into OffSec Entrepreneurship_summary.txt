The speaker, Marc Smeets, co-founder of offensive security company Outflank, presents his journey and insights into the world of entrepreneurship in the offensive security industry. He highlights the current challenges faced by the red side of infosec, including the increasing difficulty of red teaming and the impact of evolving defensive technologies. Smeets emphasizes the need for innovation and adaptation to stay ahead. He also discusses the importance of investing in tools and talent retention. The talk covers Outflank's journey, from its founding in 2016 to its eventual sale, offering lessons on branding, quality, and customer feedback. Smeets encourages attendees to embrace their ideas and take action, providing reassurance about the growing infosec market and the demand for offensive security solutions.
