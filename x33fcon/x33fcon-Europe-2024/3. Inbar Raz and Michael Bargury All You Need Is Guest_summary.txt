**Inbar Raz and Michael Bargury: All You Need Is Guest**

- Presentation on the potential security risks of granting guest access to external users in Microsoft Office 365 and other Microsoft platforms.
- Guests can gain unauthorized access to sensitive data, including SQL servers and Azure resources, by exploiting vulnerabilities and misconfigurations.
- Demonstrates how guests can set up internal phishing apps and deploy persistent backdoors.
- Emphasizes the need for secure configurations and highlights the potential abuses of Power Platform, which can inadvertently expose sensitive data.
- Introduces a newly released tool that attendees can use to apply the presented concepts.
- Discusses the implications of data breaches, including financial and reputational consequences, especially in Europe.
- Speakers encourage viewers to re-evaluate their guest access strategies and utilize the presented tools and frameworks to enhance their security posture.
