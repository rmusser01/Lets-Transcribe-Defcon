Dale Pearson, a leader in corporate red teaming, shares his expertise on building and leading a successful corporate red team. Red teaming involves simulating real-world adversary activities with a diverse group of independent individuals. He emphasizes the importance of understanding the business mission, gaining buy-in from stakeholders, and establishing trust. He suggests covering one's self by documenting processes and obtaining necessary approvals. Pearson also highlights the value of diverse talent, continuous improvement, collaboration, and effective leadership. He encourages progress over perfection and advises fighting for change to achieve excellence.
