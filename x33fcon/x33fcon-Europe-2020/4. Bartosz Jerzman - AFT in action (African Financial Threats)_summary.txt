The presentation discusses African Financial Threats (AFT), specifically cybercrime activities targeting African financial institutions. The speaker, a former member of the Polish Armed Forces and current Senior Manager for Cyber Threat Intelligence, provides an overview of unique tactics employed by AFT groups, including the use of rogue devices and insiders to gain initial access. The talk also covers the evolution of AFT groups, with a focus on keyloggers and the infrastructure of the GrabZone group. The analysis includes an investigation into an IP address associated with GrabZone, revealing insights into their tools, techniques, and targets. The speaker emphasizes the importance of threat hunting and proactive tracking, as well as the challenges faced by defenders due to corruption and the proximity of threats.
