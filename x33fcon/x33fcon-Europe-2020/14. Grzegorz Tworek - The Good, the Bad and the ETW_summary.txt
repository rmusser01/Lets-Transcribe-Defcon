Grzegorz Tworek, a malware security lead engineer, presents an overview of monitoring in Windows, with a focus on Event Tracing for Windows (ETW). ETW is a powerful tool for monitoring and debugging, offering insights into various system components. The session includes a practical demonstration of ETW's capabilities, such as sniffing USB messages and on-screen keyboard inputs. Additionally, Grzegorz explores the security aspects of ETW providers and how permissions can be manipulated. The talk concludes with a list of tools and resources for further exploration of ETW.
