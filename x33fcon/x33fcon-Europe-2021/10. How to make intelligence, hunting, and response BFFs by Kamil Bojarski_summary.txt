Kamil Bojarski, a consulting engineer at Cisco, presents a talk on the importance of aligning threat intelligence with security operations to create a cohesive and efficient system. He emphasizes the need for clear intelligence requirements and the role of the intelligence team in facilitating effective security measures. Bojarski suggests considering three factors: location, usage, and impact, to ensure smooth integration and maximize the benefit for all teams involved. He also highlights the value of bidirectional feedback loops between intelligence, hunting, and incident response teams. The presentation includes a detailed explanation of the framework and specific examples of intelligence requirements for different security teams.
