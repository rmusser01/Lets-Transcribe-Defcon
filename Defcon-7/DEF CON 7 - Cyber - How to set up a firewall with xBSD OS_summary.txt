This video from DEF CON 7 covers how to set up a firewall with xBSD O/S. The speaker covers the following topics:
- The purpose of firewalls
- Different types of firewalls
- How to avoid overloading a machine's purpose
- The importance of isolating tasks
- How to protect individual functionalities from each other
- The pros and cons of using Unix-based firewalls
- The importance of allowing services to pass through a firewall
- How to avoid users finding ways around a firewall
- The role of proxy servers
- How to protect an internal network through a firewall
- The importance of logging and how to do it securely
- How to provide services while maintaining firewall protection
- Administrative considerations for firewalls
- How to reduce privileges for better security
- How to protect against buffer overflows
