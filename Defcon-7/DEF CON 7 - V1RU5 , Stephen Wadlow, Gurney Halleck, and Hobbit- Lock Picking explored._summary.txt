This video from DEF CON 7 features V1RU5, Stephen Wadlow, Gurney Halleck, and Hobbit, who are lock-picking experts. They discuss the different types of locks and how to pick them. The video also covers the tools needed for lock picking and where to buy them. The experts also emphasise the importance of understanding how locks work and provide a disclaimer that picking handcuffs while in police custody is illegal.
